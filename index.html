About Me

I am a hungry and passionate front-end web developer, enthusiastic about immersing in an industry that is constantly evolving. Being able to create beautiful, responsive, and interactive web applications that captivate user experience and raise value in brands I admire brings me genuine gratification. My eagerness to learn combined with my resilience for conquering challenges allows me to continuously thrive and grow in this career I love.
When I do take time away from exploring this vast world of code, I like to maintain a healthy and active lifestyle. My hobbies include martial arts, yoga, basketball, hiking with my dog, and venturing through foreign countries. With my zest for fitness, I do my best to be a positive influence on others and encourage the importance of treating your body like a temple. Take a peek at my blog for some tips.

Since then I have lived in New York City and Los Angeles to manage and coordinate various hospitality and marketing events that included clients such as Lamborghini and Hermes. These past several years have given me a deeper perspective on branding and I find great joy in helping develop brand awareness and loyalty for companies I respect.
